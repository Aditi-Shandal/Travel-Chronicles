Destinations

Welcome to the enchanting Pink City! Jaipur boasts majestic palaces, historic forts, and a vibrant culture waiting to be explored.

  • Must-See: Amer Fort, City Palace, Hawa Mahal

Kerala Backwaters

Discover the tranquility of Kerala's backwaters, where you can glide on houseboats surrounded by lush greenery and palm trees.

  • Best Time to Visit: August to March
  • Must-See: Alleppey Backwaters, Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary
  • Tip: Spend a night on a houseboat to soak in the serene atmosphere, and indulge in the local seafood cuisine.

Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh

Step into one of the world's oldest cities, a spiritual hub famous for its ghats along the Ganges River and vibrant cultural rituals.

  • Best Time to Visit: October to March
  • Must-See: Dashashwamedh Ghat, Kashi Vishwanath Temple
  • Tip: Attend the mesmerizing evening Ganga Aarti and explore the narrow lanes for exquisite handicrafts and silk sarees.

Goa

Known for its stunning beaches and lively nightlife, Goa is a paradise for beach lovers and adventurers alike.

  • Best Time to Visit: November to February
  • Must-See: Baga Beach, Dudhsagar Falls, Basilica of Bom Jesus
  • Tip: Explore the serene beaches of South Goa for a peaceful escape, and relish the famous Goan seafood dishes.

Agra, Uttar Pradesh

Home to the iconic Taj Mahal, Agra is a must-visit for history enthusiasts and romantics alike.

  • Best Time to Visit: October to March
  • Must-See: Taj Mahal, Agra Fort
  • Tip: Visit the Taj Mahal at sunrise for breathtaking views and fewer crowds.

Leh-Ladakh

For the adventure seekers, Leh-Ladakh offers stunning landscapes, high-altitude lakes, and rich Buddhist culture.

  • Best Time to Visit: May to September
  • Must-See: Pangong Lake, Nubra Valley, Leh Palace
  • Tip: Acclimatize properly to the altitude and indulge in local cuisines like Thukpa and Momos.

Ranthambore National Park, Rajasthan

A wildlife enthusiast's dream, Ranthambore is known for its population of Bengal tigers and diverse flora and fauna.

  • Best Time to Visit: October to June
  • Must-See: Tiger sightings, Ranthambore Fort
  • Tip: Book your safari in advance for the best experience!

Mysore, Karnataka

Mysore is famous for its royal heritage and magnificent palaces, making it a captivating destination for history lovers.

  • Best Time to Visit: October to March
  • Must-See: Mysore Palace, Chamundi Hill
  • Tip: Don't miss the vibrant Dussehra festival held annually!

Travel Tips

Budget Travel

Travel during the off-season, book accommodations early, and rely on public transport to save money.

  • Tip: Subscribe to airline newsletters for flash sales.
  • Tip: Use apps like Airbnb for affordable lodging options.

Local Etiquette

Learn a few phrases in the local language, respect cultural norms, and always ask permission before taking photos of people.

  • Tip: A smile and courtesy can make a big difference!
  • Tip: Dress modestly when visiting religious sites.

Solo Travel

Traveling solo is a rewarding experience that allows you to explore at your own pace and meet new friends along the way.

  • Tip: Stay in hostels or join group tours to connect with fellow travelers.
  • Tip: Always share your location with someone you trust for safety.
